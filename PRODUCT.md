# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Wedding guests of Bosco Correa & Stefina Palliyil — family and friends in Kerala, India and abroad, opening a shared invitation link (mostly WhatsApp) on their phones. One-time visitors; they should feel personally invited within seconds.

## Product Purpose
A digital wedding invitation site (stefinawedsbosco.com, GitHub Pages). Two invitations live here: the main cream-watercolour invitation (`/`) and the Palliyil-family invitation (`/stephens/`), which faithfully reproduces the printed 6-page PDF ("stefinawedsbosco (600 × 800 px).pdf") — blue azulejo tile, marigold panels, lace, typewriter + script type. Success: guests read the details, feel the warmth of a hand-made keepsake, and tap "View on map" / save the date.

## Brand Personality
Handcrafted, joyful, reverent. A watercolour keepsake, not a web page — every surface should feel printed, pressed, or hand-inked. Motion is ceremonial (an envelope opened, a card lifted) rather than flashy.

## Anti-references
Generic RSVP-site templates (Zola/Minted embeds), flat Material-style cards, dark-mode anything, parallax-heavy agency sites. Nothing should read as "SaaS" or "template".

## Design Principles
1. The PDF is the art director — the web version may animate and adapt, but palette, type, and composition come from the printed piece.
2. Paper first: textures, lace edges, wax seals, film strips — physical metaphors carried faithfully into interaction.
3. One column, phone first — the invitation is a 600×800 portrait; desktop is a centred keepsake, not a layout rethink.
4. Motion tells the story of opening an envelope; every scroll reveal should feel like unwrapping, never like a UI transition.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Full prefers-reduced-motion fallback (static open envelope, native scroll). No-JS fallback must keep all content reachable. Text over marigold/tile must stay legible (deep navy ink or white with shadow).
